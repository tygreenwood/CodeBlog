October 11, 2021

Going into this project, I had a little bit of background on HTML. Primarily, this background was related to
syntax and generic structure. Like the fact that you have headers and paragraphs, and everything appears in the
order that it is coded in. What I didn't know as well was the more technical elements, like how to link other
pages, or what some of the differences between elements were. Overall, I felt that the assignment wasn't too
difficult, and the most trouble I had was figuring out how to actually publish the pages to the internet via
the engineering servers. I pretty much was able to figure everything out based on the information I got from
lectures, the one external resource I used was the Oregon State page on how to publish pages to the engineering
server.
